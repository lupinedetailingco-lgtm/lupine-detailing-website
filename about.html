About Lupine

Lupine Detailing Co is a locally owned mobile detailing service based in the Newcastle & Hunter Region. We focus on premium results, safe wash methods, and a clean, transparent experience — without the dealership price tag.

Why Choose Lupine

Local & Reliable

We’re local to the Hunter region and take pride in honest service, clear communication, and consistent results.

Mobile Convenience

We come to you. Home or workplace — we detail at your location so you can get on with your day.

Premium Method

Safe wash techniques, quality tools, and careful finishing — designed to protect your paint and interior.

Service Area

We service Newcastle & the Hunter Region. Travel within 10km of Thornton is included. Travel beyond 10km is charged at $0.75 per km.

What You Can Expect

Respectful Handling

We treat every vehicle with care. Loose items are handled respectfully and placed neatly unless you request otherwise.

Clear Communication

If we spot anything that may require extra time (heavy stains, excessive grime), we’ll contact you before proceeding.

Premium Finish

Our goal is clean, high-end results — gloss, clarity, and a cabin that feels fresh again.

Clear Service Conditions

Our pricing is based on vehicle condition and requested services. Heavily soiled vehicles or severe staining may require additional time or cost (quoted before work begins). Weather may require rescheduling. Access to power and water is preferred where possible.
